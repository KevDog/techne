# Requirements

> Full requirements spec: `docs/specs/2026-05-07-requirements-design.md`

---

## Vision

Techne is a design-phase collaboration platform for theatrical teams. Its north star is enabling productive live design meetings — a single session where the team navigates materials together should demonstrate the app's value immediately.

**Target audience:** Universities and smaller regional theaters.

**Scope:** Design phase only. Not a production execution or run-of-show tool.

---

## Core Features

- **Org & access management** — request-based org creation, invite/request membership, flexible roles
- **Show management** — org hierarchy (org → season → show → department), incremental team building
- **Materials** — upload images, files, links, and notes with rich metadata; annealing lifecycle (exploratory → proposed → decided)
- **Search** — filters + full-text at show, season, and org level
- **Meetings** — live "follow the leader" session mode; any member can claim presenter
- **Notes** — at material, show, and meeting level
- **Agent features** — optional per org; tagging, search summarization, department summaries

---

## Out of Scope — v1

Production execution tools, calendar/scheduling, budget tracking, threaded discussions, email/push notifications, PDF export, script import, native mobile app, public show pages, billing management.

---

## Success Criteria

A university theater department uses Techne for one full production and conducts at least one productive live design meeting using the app's presentation mode.
